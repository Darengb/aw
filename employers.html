Partnership

Why partner with America Works for staffing?

Hiring shouldn't mean sorting through hundreds of resumes or paying high agency fees. America Works offers a simple alternative: we recruit, screen, and support candidates—so you can focus on running your business.

01

No-fee staffing

Our services are funded through public and partner programs, so employers aren't charged for placements or support.

02

Pre-screened, job-ready candidates

We assess skills, motivation, and fit before we send candidates your way, helping you spend time only on serious applicants.

03

Retention-focused support

Many candidates complete job-readiness training and receive months of mentoring after hire, helping them make a smooth transition and stay on the job.

04

Tax credit & compliance opportunities

A large share of our candidates qualify for federal incentives like the Work Opportunity Tax Credit and can help you meet federal contractor diversity and disability hiring goals.

Process

How our staffing process works.

We keep the process straightforward for you and supportive for your new hires.

01

Tell us what you're hiring for

Start by submitting a job order or talking with our team about your open roles, locations, and requirements—skills, schedule, experience level, and workplace environment.

02

We source and screen candidates

Our staff recruits from our talent pool and partner programs, then pre-screens candidates to confirm interest, availability, and fit. Only motivated, qualified candidates are forwarded to you.

03

You choose who to hire

We coordinate interviews—at your site or virtually. You make the final hiring decision. Many employers work with us repeatedly to fill ongoing staffing needs.

04

We stay involved after hire

After a candidate starts, our team checks in regularly, offers coaching, and helps them navigate challenges. This extra layer of support helps your new employee settle in and stay productive.

Preparation

Candidates prepared to succeed—not just to show up.

Many candidates participate in a structured job-readiness program that covers communication, customer service, workplace expectations, and basic computer and soft skills before they ever meet with you. That means they arrive at interviews with a clearer understanding of what it takes to be successful on the job.

After hire, we stay in touch with your new employee for a period of time—offering mentoring, guidance, and problem-solving support during and outside of work hours. This reduces early turnover and helps employees stay engaged and dependable.
