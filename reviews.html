Usage Recommendations

Style 1: Cards with Stars

Use for dedicated testimonial sections. Works well in 2-3 column grids. Good for showing multiple reviews at once.

Style 2: Simple Inline

Sprinkle between content sections. Subtle but effective. Best for short, punchy quotes that don't interrupt flow.
